How to play:

This game has three simple keystrokes to move your player. 

1) Move right: To move right, depress the right arrow key on the key board.

2) Move Left: To move left, depress the left arrow key on the key board.

3) Jump: To jump, depress the Up arrow key on the key board.

WARNING!! There are times where the jump function will not activate if the right or left arrow keys are pressed down!
	If you run into this problem and have a fix for it please leave a comment with how to fix it!

WARNING!! There are two platforms in the game that are not what they seem, and you may fall through! 
		Be careful, pay attention, and have fun!
